# Cookie Policy

**Contact:** tuarone@gmail.com  
**Effective date:** 2026-02-23

## 1. What are cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies (local storage, pixels) may be used for related purposes.

## 2. Categories we use

### A) Essential cookies (always on)
Required for core functionality and security, for example:
- session handling / authentication state;
- CSRF/security protection;
- load balancing/basic technical operation;
- storing consent preferences.

Without essential cookies, the site may not function correctly.

### B) Optional analytics cookies (opt-in)
Used only if you consent, for example:
- aggregate traffic statistics;
- feature usage and performance metrics;
- error trend analysis.

These help improve product quality in beta, but are not required for basic use.

## 3. Legal basis
- Essential cookies: legitimate interest / necessity for service operation.
- Optional analytics cookies: your consent.

## 4. Managing cookie preferences
You can:
- accept or reject optional analytics cookies via the cookie banner/settings;
- change preferences later (if settings panel is available);
- delete cookies in your browser.

## 5. Third-party services
If analytics providers are enabled, limited technical data may be processed by those providers under their own terms.

## 6. Retention
Cookie duration varies by type (session-only or persistent for a limited period). We aim to minimize storage duration where practical.

## 7. Updates
This policy may be updated as the product evolves.
